#1.Yello is a campaign about mental awareness with the aim to spread a positive energy in the drive to assist people to change their mental directions.

1 is associated to the colour yellow and the #1.Yello campaign aims to socially spread yellow throughout digital and social media channels such as Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.

Why the campaign? See for yourself.

Mental health affects people in many ways, some experience mild occurrences while others truly suffer deeply with issues affecting their every day lives.

The campaign is to assist people to overcome their fears by sharing their innermost thoughts. They are able to witness that others share similar experiences and that they are not alone. By sharing their fears, others are able to help them overcome their problems.
